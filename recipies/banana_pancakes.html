Best Banana Pancakes in the World

Healthy and fluffy, they will seize your heart in one bite

These pancakes are simply incredible, the light taste of banana is game changer and pairs very well with a Nutella Toping.

But whats even better is you can eat them as much as you want without worrying about your belly fat because there is no butter in it !

So don't restrain and have a great 'Gouter' as we say in french :D

Ingredients

Here is the recipe for 4 people. If you want more just do the math as for the moment I didn't learn to embed code in a website hehe

  • 2 Bananas (of course)
  • 2 Eggs
  • 200g of Flour
  • 70g of Sugar (don't hesitate to put less if you're a gym bro)
  • 1 teaspon of Baking Powder
  • 10cl of Milk
  • 1 pinch of salt

Recipe Steps

  1. Smash the bananas roughly in a bowl
  2. Add the beaten eggs, the sugar, the salt, and the baking powder, then mix the mixture
  3. Add the flour and mix again
  4. Add the milk while continuing to mix, the mixture should be smickyyyy
  5. Leave it to rest for 10 minutes
  6. In a pan slightly oiled, pour small amounts of mixture in circles without making them touch
  7. Turn the pancakes after approximatively 1 minute to cook the other side
  8. Enjoy your pancakes
